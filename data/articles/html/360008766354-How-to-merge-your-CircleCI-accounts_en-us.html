When you sign in to CircleCI, you do so via OAuth with one selected VCS provider (example: Github). From there, you can add additional account integrations (example: BitBucket) on your account's page here: https://circleci.com/account

If you run into a situation where you have linked an account previously and are trying to connect it to another account you will see this error:

We encountered a problem adding your identity to CircleCI... The identity provided by your VCS service is already associated with a different account. Please try logging in using that account or contact us for further help.

If you receive this error, contact Support at the link below and we can help you unlink the accounts, so they may be rejoined as you wish.

https://support.circleci.com/hc/en-us/requests/new
